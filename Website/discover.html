Problem Statement

Employing a candidate is a lengthy and complex process involving many verifications throughout the process. This takes too much time and in a saturated industry where applications to a job exceeds more than 100 candidates makes it impossible for the Company to check and verify everyone. According to “resources.workable.com” it takes on average about 28 days to finally clear and verify a person for a successful hiring. Moreover, keeping a transparent record of the professional career of a student or a former employee is also a real world problem. Following time-taking steps are the reason present manual verification method is not feasible

  • Education Verification
  • Identity Verification
  • Address Check
  • Reference Check

Features

  • Employers at open house could search for specific projects and specific skills they want from our app/website without any hassle of verifying or testing the candidate themselves
  • Coursera/Udemy projects can also be added to the University's Blockchain after proper evaluation by respective instructors.
  • University own repository ( like Github ) can be integrated with the Blockchain system to keep a thorough history of what and when the student made the project so that the same project can be used in Future.
  • Verifiable CV’s can be given to GlassDoor so that international employers can also easily verify local employees.
